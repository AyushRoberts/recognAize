About RECOGNAIZE

recognAIze is built on the foundation of JS with the aim of providing fast and easily accessible AI facial recognition features to users on the go. Built on Tensorflow.js, recognAIzer runs on most modern browsers without requiring the user to downlaod any external library or dependancy



Artificial Intelligence is , the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think like humans.

Age and gender are the two most fundamental facial qualities in social interaction.
The aim is to predict the age of individuals using image data sets.
It is important as -

  • Helpful in development of smart human-machine interfaces.
  • Matching people as friends based on preferences
  • Simplifying long-distance meetings (coming soon)
